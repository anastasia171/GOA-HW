information about
the first ever car:

The first-ever car is widely recognized as the Benz Patent-Motorwagen, built by Karl Benz in 1885 and patented in 1886. This vehicle is considered the world's first automobile designed to be propelled by an internal combustion engine. In 1888, Karl Benz's wife, Bertha Benz, famously undertook the first long-distance automobile trip, driving the Motorwagen 106 km (66 miles) to demonstrate its viability and practicality. This invention laid the foundation for the modern automobile industry.

daihatsu cuore (L201)

basic overview:

The Daihatsu Cuore (L201) is a small, compact car produced by Daihatsu, a Japanese automaker, as part of the Cuore (or Mira) lineup. The L201 model is a second-generation Cuore, manufactured between 1985 and 1990, succeeding the L55 and preceding the L200. It was widely popular for its affordability, compact size, and fuel efficiency, making it a suitable urban vehicle.
